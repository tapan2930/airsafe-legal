Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026

AirSafe ("the App") is an offline password manager and authenticator application developed by Tapan ("Developer", "we", "us", or "our"). Your privacy is fundamentally important to us, and this policy explains how the App handles your information.

Key principle: AirSafe operates 100% offline. No data ever leaves your device. We do not collect, transmit, store, or have access to any of your information.

1. Information We Collect

None. AirSafe does not collect any personal data, usage data, analytics, or telemetry of any kind. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers.
  • We do not collect usage statistics or behavioral analytics.
  • We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.
  • We do not access your contacts, location, microphone, or any other device sensors beyond the camera (for QR code scanning, used solely on-device).

2. Data Storage

All data you enter into AirSafe — including passwords, TOTP secrets, notes, and app settings — is stored exclusively in an encrypted local database on your device.

  • Encryption: Your vault is protected by AES-256 encryption. The encryption key is derived from your master password using PBKDF2 with 100,000 iterations.
  • Biometrics: If you enable biometric unlock, your encryption key is stored in the Android Keystore system (hardware-backed secure storage). We never see or store your biometric data.
  • No cloud sync: There is no cloud storage, no remote servers, and no network communication of any kind.

3. Camera Permission

AirSafe requests camera access solely to scan QR codes for adding TOTP authenticator entries. Camera input is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.

4. Autofill Service (Android)

AirSafe can act as an Android autofill provider. When enabled, the operating system may request credential suggestions from the App to fill login forms in other apps and browsers. This communication happens locally between the Android system and AirSafe on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

5. Data Export & Import

AirSafe allows you to export your vault to an encrypted .airsafe file and import from backup files or Google Password Manager CSV files. These operations are performed locally. Exported files are saved to your device storage — you are responsible for the security of exported files.

6. Clipboard

When you copy a password or TOTP code, it is placed in your device clipboard. AirSafe provides an auto-clear feature that automatically removes copied data from the clipboard after a configurable timeout (default: 30 seconds).

7. Third-Party Services

AirSafe does not integrate with any third-party services, advertising networks, analytics providers, or social media platforms. The App makes zero network requests.

8. Children's Privacy

AirSafe does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Since the App collects no data whatsoever, it is compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.

9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times:

  • Delete individual entries from within the App (moved to Trash, permanently deleted after 30 days or manually).
  • Uninstalling the App removes all associated data from your device.
  • Clear app data from Android Settings to reset everything.

10. Security

We take security seriously and have implemented the following measures:

  • AES-256 encrypted database with PBKDF2 key derivation.
  • Auto-lock after configurable timeout.
  • Optional screenshot protection (prevents the app from appearing in screenshots and Recent Apps).
  • Automatic clipboard clearing.
  • No network access — eliminates remote attack vectors entirely.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Since the App does not collect data, we have no way to notify you of changes — please review this policy periodically.
